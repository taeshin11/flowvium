## 구현 (src/app/api/price-history/route.ts)
- `fetchStooqDaily()` → `fetchYahooDaily()` 로 치환.
- URL: `https://query1.finance.yahoo.com/v8/finance/chart/{ticker}?interval=1d&range={range}`
- Range mapping: 5d / 1mo / 3mo / 6mo / 1y — days 파라미터에 가장 잘 맞는 것 선택.
- UA 헤더 필수: `Mozilla/5.0 (compatible; Flowvium/1.0)` — 기본 Node fetch UA 는 reject 당함.
- Response schema: `{chart:{result:[{timestamp:[], indicators:{quote:[{close:[]}]}}]}}`
- Unix timestamp → ISO YYYY-MM-DD 변환.
