```
Generate ONLY catalysts (2 items) and fundamentalBasis (1 line) for ${ticker}.
Live price: ${ccy}${lp}
Sector / Insider / Squeeze / YoY / Margin signals

⚠️ RULES:
- Catalysts specific to THIS ticker (NOT generic sector talk)
- Each ≤ 60 chars. Cite actual signal numbers.
- fundamentalBasis ≤ 80 chars
- NO future-quarter speculation
- NO cross-ticker bleed

Pure JSON: {"catalysts":[...],"fundamentalBasis":"..."}
```
