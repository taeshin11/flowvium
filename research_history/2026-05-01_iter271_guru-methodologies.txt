## 변경 파일

### 신규: src/lib/guru-methodologies.ts
- GuruCriteria 인터페이스 정의
- GURU_METHODOLOGIES: 8명 구루의 매수/매도 원칙 DB
  - Warren Buffett (value): ROE>15%, Debt/Equity<0.5, Owner earnings yield>2x 10Y treasury
  - Peter Lynch (growth): PEG<1, Revenue growth>15% YoY, EPS growth 20-50%
  - Joel Greenblatt (value): Magic Formula EBIT/EV>10% + ROIC>25%
  - Benjamin Graham (value): Graham Number = sqrt(22.5 x EPS x Book Value)
  - Philip Fisher (growth): Scuttlebutt, R&D/Sales, Profit margin 5Y
  - Howard Marks (value): Second-level thinking, Market cycle, Risk premium
  - Stanley Druckenmiller (macro): Liquidity-driven, Earnings inflection, Fed easing
  - Michael Burry (value): P/FCF<10, Insider buying, Share buybacks
- getGuruPromptContext(): top5 구루 핵심 신호를 AI 프롬프트 텍스트로 반환
